        <h2>Radio</h2>
<h3>OzoneChannel</h3>

        <p>
          <img src="/img/ozone-channel-logo.svg" class="logo" alt="OzoneChannel logo" title="OzoneChannel" />
        </p>

<p>OzoneChannel is one of the first radio shows about club culture and electronic music in Russia, founded in 1997 by OzonePro team on Kazan radio station BIM Radio.</p>

<p>Having instilled a taste for good music in more than one generation of clubbers, OzoneChannel continues to be broadcast with enviable consistency for more than 25 years.</p>

<p>The hosts of the program are DJs and sound producers: Shamil OM, Kutuzov and me.</p>

   <p>&nbsp;</p>

      <h4>How to listen OzoneChannel live</h4>
<p>The program is aired every Monday at 11 p.m. on BIM-radio (102.8 FM in Kazan and other cities).</p>
<p>You can listen to the live broadcast via the BIM-radio App (available on the <a href="https://itunes.apple.com/ru/app/%D0%B1%D0%B8%D0%BC-%D1%80%D0%B0%D0%B4%D0%B8%D0%BE/id1164043173?mt=8&ign-mpt=uo%3D4">App Store</a> and <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=ru.bimradio.lpi&hl=ru">Play Market</a>), the <a href="http://ozonechannel.ru/">OzoneChannel website</a> or radio on 102.8FM (in Kazan or <a href="https://www.bimradio.ru/about/local/">another cities</a>).</p>
